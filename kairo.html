KAIRO

The Visionary / Producer-Maknae

Human. Neural modder. Audio-reactive enhancements. Kairo’s brain doesn’t run like everyone else’s. Known as “Livewire Syndrome.” Always too many thoughts, too fast.

He sees music — literally. Frequencies shape his world. And he shapes them back.

He wired the band’s first drop from a scrapyard with a cigarette lighter and a half-dead tablet.

One of his baselines once hijacked an entire city block. He shrugged and said, ‘Good baseline, though.’

Genius. Chaos-gremlin. Legend-in-progress.
